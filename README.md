# ARCHIVE OF ECHOES: THE ANUNNAKI LEGACY

> **Fantasy Label**: This is a work of speculative myth-fantasy. All content is fictional and uses mythology, ancient architecture, and historical motifs as creative ingredients. No factual or historical claims are made.

---

## What It Is

A **mythic puzzle comic game** for iPhone (portrait, touch-only).

In the year 2100, a lone explorer finds a forbidden analog comic in a forgotten archive. The moment he touches it — he is pulled inside. To escape, he must complete fractured panels across mythic timelines. As the story assembles itself, a hidden convergence emerges: an unfinished Pyramid. A missing artifact. A final panel that will either free him — or remake the world.

---

## One-Sentence Pitch

> *In 2100, a man is pulled into a mystic comic; he must solve panel-puzzles across broken timelines, discover the hidden Pyramid-Ark convergence, complete the final spread, and escape back to a utopian future.*

---

## Core Pillars

| Pillar | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| The comic IS the world | Pages = levels. Panels = rooms. Gutters = secrets. |
| Puzzles unlock panels | No kill-count. Knowledge is the progression system. |
| Branching with convergence | All paths lead to the same finale — differently. |
| Late revelation | The Pyramid + Ark is discovered, not assigned. |
| Escape = Win | The goal is freedom. The reward is utopia. |
