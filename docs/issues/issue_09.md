## Issue Summary

The player is now inside the structure named Giza. This is not the first time they've been here — Issue 07 explored a partial interior. Now they have the complete cross-section map (from Issue 08's 5-page Overlay), the Ark's name, and one component. This is the full dungeon.

Three internal levels:
1. **Ground level**: Entry passage, first junction point, connection to the descending shaft
2. **King's chamber level**: The central chamber (seen in Issue 07) — this time, with the Ark's full schema visible and circuit architecture illuminated
3. **Apex approach**: The shaft from the King's chamber that leads directly to the capstone position — a vertical climb puzzle

At the top: the capstone slot. Empty. The player sees the slot clearly — the correct proportions for the capstone are encoded in the walls around it. The Capstone Placement puzzle (E4) is shown as an intro here: the slot exists, but the capstone is not yet in hand. That comes in Issue 12.

## Page 4 — "Level 2: Cardinal Alignment Final" (Puzzle: C1 Final)

**Format**: Full-page puzzle

**Puzzle type**: C1 Cardinal Alignment (final, hardest)
- The chamber's internal compass positions are shown alongside the star map
- Four shaft openings in the chamber walls (north, south, east, west — approximately) must each be aligned to their corresponding stellar targets
- Each shaft has a movable capstone that adjusts its angle slightly

**On completion**: The coffer glows — it's responding to the light alignment. The circuit diagram in the Spiritual Lens resolves from partial to complete (except for the capstone node, which remains dark).

## Design Notes

- **Issue 09 as "arrival" issue**: After 8 issues of assembling context, Issue 09 is the first issue where the player's destination has been reached. The tone should feel different — not investigative, but homecoming. They know this place.
- **Three-level shaft labyrinth** is the gameplay highpoint for the C2 mechanic. The shaft door mechanic adds interactive logic (not just navigation) to the labyrinth.
- **Circuit architecture (Spiritual Lens)** is the visual payoff for a mechanic that's been accumulating since Issue 04. The circuit connects everything in one visual — and the incomplete capstone/Ark positions are immediately visible in it.
- **Capstone slot preview (E4 intro)** gives the player a concrete destination for Issue 12. The tracing gesture performed here is slightly different from the actual placement gesture in Issue 12 — tracing the outline vs. placing the object — which creates continuity and escalation.
- **Scribe maximum + Resonants counter** establishes a new normal for Issues 10–12: the Scribes are fully mobilized, but the Resonants are actively assisting. The Scribes can't stop what's coming alone.
